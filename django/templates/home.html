blankit

Lauryn Anderson


blankit is a fill-in-the-blank game generator.

Powered by SpaCy, it takes any paragraph and turns it into a MadLibs-style game where players suggest arbitrary words to fill in the blanks, producing hilarious nonsense.

This implementation fulfills the course project requirements in the UCalgary CPSC 599.27 Natural Language Processing course, taught by Dr. Katie Ovens in Winter 2023.
